Overview

The FRAM Data Warehouse aims to provide a single location where users can access and download all data collected by the NOAA Northwest Fisheries Science Center (NWFSC) Fishery Resource Analysis & Monitoring (FRAM) division.

This includes data from the following FRAM programs/surveys:

  • Acoustics Survey - Integrated Hake Acoustics Survey
  • Economics & Social Science Research (ESSR)
  • Hook & Line Survey - Southern California Hook & Line Survey
  • Marine Habitat Program
  • Observer Program - West Coast Groundfish Observer Program (WCGOP)
  • Trawl Survey - West Coast Groundfish Bottom Trawl Survey (WCGBTS)

Most API calls do not require a login.
